Our Yog Guru Shri Kaushal

Our founder Shri Kaushal, believes that our thoughts shape our whole experience. When the mind is relaxed and in balance, the body naturally follows. Hence,Samattva School was founded to offer a journey inward, where yoga becomes a pathway to mental peace, physical harmony, and emotional well-being.

He is a born mystic. From the age of 7, he has been experiencing unexplained connection with energy, nature and supernatural phenomenas. Soon he realised his bigger purpose in life to spread the gems of our ancient sciences and bring wellness and happiness to all. He started his further journey towards this goal of absolute bliss through the path of Balance i.e. Samattva.

The true purpose of yoga education, as he envisioned, is to create a world free from negativities,and replacing with love, compassion, and well-being. Through the message of Sarve Bhavantu Sukhinah—may all be happy—Samattva fosters a balanced approach to life, helping individuals reconnect with their own true nature that is Eternal, Invincible, and Blissful.
